Mack Brown's Longhorns

The man, the myth, the legend!

Here are some facts about Mack's time at UT:

  • 12-0 (2005) - Mack had a perfect season in 2004-2005 and capped it off with a national title.
  • 10-4 - This is the bowl record under Mack at Texas. The Longhorns were also winners of 3-1 in BCS bowls.
  • 11 - The number of consecutive seasons with a winning record while at Texas (1998-2008).
  • 8 - The number of consecutive 10+ win seasons for Mack at Texas (2000-2008).
  • Vince Young - Became one of the best collegiate quarterbacks football has ever known.
  • Colt McCoy - Became the second best quarterback Texas had ever had.
  • 21 - Record setting consecutive conference wins from 2004-2006.
  • 28-16 - Brown's record against rival Big 12 teams OU, TTU, and TAMU.
